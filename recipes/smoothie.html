Raspberry Blackberry Smoothie

A delicious smoothie!

Very tasty cold beverage for hot days or just to start the day on the right foot.

  • 1 Small Banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1(6 ounces) container vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 4 ice cubes
  1. Place banana, blackberries, raspberries, yogurt, honey, and ice cubes into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
